# Mycobacterium Tuberculosis Transcription Factors

Data about the targets and gene expression impacts of *Mycobacterium
tuberculosis* (Mtb) transcription factors (TF). Data from
[Rustad et al. 2014](https://doi.org/10.1186/s13059-014-0502-3)

## References

- Rustad, T. R., Minch, K. J., Ma, S., Winkler, J. K., Hobbs, S., Hickey, M.,
  Brabant, W., Turkarslan, S., Price, N. D., Baliga, N. S., & Sherman, D. R.
  (2014). Mapping and manipulating the Mycobacterium tuberculosis transcriptome
  using a transcription factor overexpression-derived regulatory network. Genome
  Biology, 15(11), 502.
  [https://doi.org/10.1186/s13059-014-0502-3](https://doi.org/10.1186/s13059-014-0502-3)
